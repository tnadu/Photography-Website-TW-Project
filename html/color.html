General Information

This website was designed as a class assignment for the CS Web Course of the 'University of Bucharest'. With the exception of the logo image, all of the media content is original and was produced as part of the project, while the logo is royalty-free and was therefore aquired legally. The purpose of this website is to showcase some of my personal photography work, but it is also a meaningful template for promoting various other types of digital art. The photos on display are intended to be a snapshot of my portofolio and have been taken over the span of 5 years.
